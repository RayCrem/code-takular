Turkey Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 Turkey Slices
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Small Onion(Diced)
  • 1 Red bell pepper(Diced)
  • 1/8 cup of Milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat skillet, to medium-heat.
  2. blend together all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. simmer low. Flipping once, until brown.
  4. serve to
    taste
    with salt and pepper.
