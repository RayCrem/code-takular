Turkey Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 Turkey Slices
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of Milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat skillet, to medium.
  2. blend together all ingredients in a bowl
  3. cook low. Flipping until brown.
  4. serve to taste with
    cheese,
    salt, pepper.
