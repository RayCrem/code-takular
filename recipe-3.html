Skillet Steak

Ingredients

  • 12oz Top Sirloin (a lean cut of beef)
  • 2 teaspoon(grilling seasonings)
  • 3 cups of mushrooms
  • 2 cloves of garlic(minced)
  • 2 cups frozen peas
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 6oz trimmed broccolini
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons Avocado oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F. Sprinkle steak with grilling seasoning, garlic.
  2. Heat oil in a cast-iron skillet over med-heat. Add steak and broccolini.
  3. Cook 4 min turning broccolini(not the steak). Place peas in skillet.Transfer skillet to oven.
  4. Bake at med-high(145°F)for 8 minutes.Transfer steak and vegetables to a plate and leave the rest of the liquid in the pan.
  5. Cook over med heat.In a small bowl whisk together cornstarch and beef broth adding mushrooms
  6. Simmer for 1-2 min pour onto steak and serve.
